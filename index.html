Staten Island Mall

The staten Island Mall is lowkey kinda fun. Its pretty Massive. Massive? Anyways, There is a new massive food court section with a Dave & Busters, which is rally tuff. If there is a main spot to hang on the island this is the place. Plus all the Old Staten Islanders wont be there at any hour of the day.

Snug Harbor

Snug Harbor is absolutely beautiful. It is a community Garden and Arts center and is an awesome place to throw a frizbee or just visit on a beautiful day. This might be the only universally acceptable spot on Statn Island. Nobody can really deny that Snug Harbor is nice. There is also a hidden little castle with a garden maze in the back which I always thought was cool since I was young.
