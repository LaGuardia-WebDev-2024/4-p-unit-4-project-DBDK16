My Unofficial Local Guide to Manhattan Beach

Coney Island Park

Coney Island amusement park is the staple of brighton beach, which is right next to my neighborhood. Friends who visit from far away love to spend time there, especially when it gets dark. I also love to go there with my best friend at the beginning of july, when the weather is perfect and the park is not as popular.



Orso Coffee

Orso Coffee is my favorite coffee shop. They make the best Chai and Matcha Lattes. I recommend anyone who visits my neighborhood to go get blessed by their amazing handcrafted beverages.



Manhattan Beach

Manhattan beach is the name of my neighborhood, but is also the name of our beach. I love going to the beach all year long, whether I am walking my dog or tanning in the sun. Anyone who visits my neighborhood usually checks out the beach.



In summary, make sure to visit:

  • Luna Park
  • Orso Coffee
  • Manhattan Beach
